## Complete Triangle Geometry Solver
Triangles form the foundational basis of geometry, architecture, and trigonometry. However, manually computing missing sides, angles, and area using the Law of Sines, Law of Cosines, or Heron's Formula can be tedious and prone to mathematical errors.

Our **Triangle Calculator** is engineered to instantly solve any triangle based on the minimal known variables you have. 

### Calculation Modes
We have structured the engine into three distinct modes to perfectly match your available data:

1.  **3 Sides Known (SSS):** Enter the lengths of all three sides. The engine will utilize the Law of Cosines to extract the exact internal angles, and deploy Heron's formula to compute the exact overall area and perimeter.
2.  **2 Sides & Included Angle (SAS):** Enter two side lengths and the angle enclosed between them. The calculator will instantly determine the opposing third side and the remaining two angles.
3.  **Base & Height:** The classic methodology. If you simply need the area of a mathematical plane, provide the base and the perpendicular height to instantly compute half the bounding box (Area = ½ × base × height).
