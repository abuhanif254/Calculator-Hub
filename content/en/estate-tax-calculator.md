# Estate Tax Calculator: Planning Your Legacy

An estate tax is a financial levy on an heir's inherited portion of an estate if the value of the estate exceeds an exclusion limit set by law. Planning for this tax is a critical part of wealth preservation and ensuring your legacy is passed on according to your wishes.

## How Estate Tax Works

Estate taxes are distinct from inheritance taxes. While estate taxes are calculated on the total value of the deceased's assets, inheritance taxes are typically paid by the beneficiary receiving the assets.

### The Calculation Formula

To estimate your potential liability, we use the following architectural model:
1. **Gross Estate**: The fair market value of everything you own (Real estate, bank accounts, stocks, business interests, life insurance proceeds).
2. **Adjusted Gross Estate**: Gross Estate minus debts, mortgages, and funeral expenses.
3. **Taxable Estate**: Adjusted Gross Estate minus charitable bequests and the marital deduction (assets left to a surviving spouse).
4. **Tax Liability**: The Taxable Estate minus the **Unified Credit/Exclusion Limit** (The amount exempt from tax), multiplied by the progressive tax rate.

## 2024 US Federal Estate Tax Guidelines

In the United States, the federal estate tax exclusion for 2024 is **$13.61 million** per individual. This means most estates fall well below the threshold and owe $0 in federal estate taxes. However, for those exceeding this limit, the top tax rate is currently **40%**.

### Key Planning Strategies

*   **Lifetime Gifting**: You can reduce the size of your taxable estate by gifting assets during your lifetime (up to $18,000 per recipient annually in 2024 without using your lifetime exclusion).
*   **Irrevocable Life Insurance Trusts (ILITs)**: By holding life insurance in a trust, the death benefit can be excluded from your gross estate.
*   **Charitable Lead/Remainder Trusts**: These instruments allow you to support charities while providing income to yourself or your heirs, often with significant tax benefits.
