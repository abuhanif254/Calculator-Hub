## Managing Fractional Math
Fractions can be notoriously tricky to compute, especially when adding or subtracting values with different denominators (which requires calculating the Least Common Multiple) or dealing with large mixed numbers.

Our **Global Fraction Calculator** is engineered to bypass manual calculation errors while providing mathematically perfect simplifications and decimal equivalents.

### How to Compute Fractions
The calculator supports two complete fractional units interacting through four primary operators:
*   **Addition (+):** Finds a common denominator to sum parts together.
*   **Subtraction (-):** Aligns denominators to find the exact difference between two fractional values.
*   **Multiplication (×):** Multiplies numerators together and denominators together, bypassing the need for common denominators.
*   **Division (÷):** Mathematically executes by taking the reciprocal (flipping) the second fraction and multiplying.

### Proper vs. Improper Fractions
This engine supports inputs as **Mixed Numbers** (e.g., `2 1/4`). When calculated, it automatically normalizes everything to improper fractions under the hood, runs the mathematical operation, and then cleanly simplifies the result back out into a standard fraction or Mixed Number depending on its heaviness. It also provides the exact decimal conversion (e.g., `2.25`) for seamless cross-industry integration.
