## What is a Body Fat Calculator?
Knowing your overall weight isn't enough to determine true physical health or athletic progress. Understanding your **Body Fat Percentage** allows you to accurately measure exactly how much of your weight is composed of adipose tissue (fat) versus lean body mass (muscles, bones, organs, and water).

Our **Online Body Fat Calculator** utilizes the mathematically proven **U.S. Navy Method**, which requires only highly accessible tape measurements instead of expensive dual-energy X-ray absorptiometry (DEXA) scans or water displacement tanks.

### How to use this Calculator:
1.  **Select Your Standards:** Choose Metric (cm, kg) or Imperial (inches, lbs).
2.  **Input Biometrics:** Enter your age, gender, and current weight and overall height. 
3.  **Provide Circumference Measurements:**
    *   **Neck:** Measure just below the larynx (Adam's apple). Keep the tape flat.
    *   **Waist:** Men should measure at the navel. Women should measure at the narrowest point of the abdomen.
    *   **Hip (Women Only):** Measure at the widest protrusion of the hips and buttocks.
4.  **Analyze Your Output:** The engine computes the exact algorithm to display your Body Fat Percentage, exactly how many pounds/kilos of fat you carry, and your total Lean Body Mass.

### Understanding Body Fat Categories:
The American Council on Exercise (ACE) establishes the following categorizations:
*   **Essential Fat:** Men: 2–5% | Women: 10–13%
*   **Athletes:** Men: 6–13% | Women: 14–20%
*   **Fitness:** Men: 14–17% | Women: 21–24%
*   **Average:** Men: 18–24% | Women: 25–31%
*   **Obese:** Men: 25%+ | Women: 32%+
