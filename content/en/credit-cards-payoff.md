## What is a Credit Card Payoff Calculator?
A **Credit Card Payoff Calculator** is an essential financial tool that reveals exactly how long it will take to eliminate your credit card debt based on your current balance, interest rate (APR), and monthly payment amount.

By inputting your numbers, you can visualize the timeline to becoming debt-free and understand the massive impact of interest charges over time.

### Why Credit Card Debt is So Expensive
Credit cards use compound interest, which means you pay interest on your interest. If you only make the minimum payment each month, it can take years (or even decades) to clear a balance, while the total interest paid often eclipses the original amount borrowed.

### How to Use This Tool
1.  **Enter your Total Balance:** The total amount you currently owe.
2.  **Enter your Interest Rate (APR):** Your Annual Percentage Rate (e.g., 19.99%).
3.  **Choose your Goal:** You can calculate based on a fixed **Monthly Payment** you can afford, or calculate the payment needed to pay off the debt in a specific number of **Months**.

### The Power of Extra Payments
Even adding an extra $50 a month to your payment can drastically reduce both the time it takes to become debt-free and the amount of interest the bank gets to keep. Use this calculator to experiment with different payment amounts and build your personal debt repayment strategy.
