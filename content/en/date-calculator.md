## What is a Date Calculator?
Our **Online Date Calculator** is a precision tool designed to perform complex mathematical calendar operations. Our Gregorian calendar system is infamously difficult to calculate manually due to unequal month lengths, leap years, and temporal offsets. 

This calculator completely automates two distinct temporal functions:
1.  **Calculate Duration Between Dates:** Instantly determine the exact span of time between a Start Date and an End Date.
2.  **Add or Subtract from a Date:** Provide a starting date and precisely add or subtract a specific number of years, months, weeks, or days to find a target future or past baseline.

### How Days Between Dates Works
To calculate the duration between a start and end point, our calculator relies on programmatic timestamps measuring elapsed milliseconds. This perfectly factors in all leap years (intercalary days). The outcome is instantly formatted into three views:
*   Total absolute days (e.g., *1,042 days*)
*   Total calendar span (e.g., *2 years, 10 months, 15 days*)
*   Alternative structural data (weeks + days)

### Alternative Applications
This tool is heavily used for:
*   **Legal Contracts:** Adding 30, 45, or 60-day deadlines to a signature date.
*   **Project Management:** Calculating exact workflow duration windows.
*   **Event Planning:** Finding exactly how many days remain until a wedding/vacation.
*   **Scientific Timelines:** Tracing absolute historical durations.

### Leap Year Adjustments
If you leap over February 29th during an operation, the engine's internal calendar library securely adjusts the math. Therefore, adding "1 Year" to February 29th will land gracefully on February 28th of the standard subsequent year.
