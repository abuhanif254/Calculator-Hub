## What is a Time Calculator?
Time mathematics can be incredibly frustrating to calculate manually because it utilizes a base-60 (sexagesimal) and base-24 system rather than standard base-10 decimals. Our **Time Calculator** handles the complex conversions between days, hours, minutes, and seconds automatically. 

This engine is split into two primary tools:
1.  **Time Difference:** Instantly determine exactly how many hours and minutes have elapsed between a specific Start Time and End Time. 
2.  **Add / Subtract Time Duration:** Mathematically combine two distinct time spans (e.g., adding *4 hours and 45 minutes* to *3 hours and 30 minutes*).

### Finding the Difference Between Times
Used frequently for payroll mapping, shift tracking, and flight duration logic, this tool looks at two clock times and extracts the exact duration. 

**Cross-Midnight Calculations:**
If you enter a Start Time of `10:00 PM` and an End Time of `06:00 AM`, the calculator's internal clock algorithm mathematically recognizes that the end time has crossed midnight into a new day, successfully outputting an exact *8 Hour* duration without requiring you to manually attach calendar dates.

### Adding and Subtracting Durations
If you are tracking billable project hours or attempting to sum up audio/video track lengths, you can use the Add/Subtract calculator to merge the payloads.

Because time doesn't scale in factors of 100, adding *45 minutes* and *35 minutes* results in *80 minutes*, which the calculator will instantly format perfectly into *1 Hour and 20 Minutes*. All values instantly cascade up from seconds into minutes, minutes into hours, and hours into days perfectly.
