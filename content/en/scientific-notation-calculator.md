# Scientific Notation Calculator

Our free Scientific Notation Calculator makes it easy to work with very large or very small numbers. Whether you need to convert a standard decimal number into scientific notation (often called standard form), or you need to add, subtract, multiply, or divide numbers already in scientific or E-notation, this tool provides instant and accurate results.

### What is Scientific Notation?
Scientific notation is a way of expressing numbers that are too big or too small to be conveniently written in decimal form. It is commonly used by scientists, mathematicians, and engineers. 

A number is written in scientific notation when a number between 1 and 10 is multiplied by a power of 10.
For example, the number 5,000,000 can be written as `5 × 10^6`. In E-notation (often used on calculators and computers), this same number is written as `5e6`.

### How to use the Converter
1. Select the **Converter** tab.
2. Enter your number in the field. You can enter a standard decimal number (e.g., `123456`) or a number in E-notation (e.g., `1.23e5`).
3. Click "Convert". The calculator will display your number in proper Scientific Notation, E-notation, and as a full decimal number.

### How to use Math Operations
1. Select the **Math Operations** tab.
2. Enter your first number in either decimal or E-notation.
3. Choose the mathematical operation: Addition, Subtraction, Multiplication, or Division.
4. Enter your second number.
5. Click "Calculate" to see the result. The calculator automatically handles the exponents and provides the answer in both scientific notation and standard decimal form.
