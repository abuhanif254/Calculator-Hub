## What is a Grade Calculator?
The **Grade Calculator** is an essential academic tool designed for students to track their exact standing in a class. Instead of guessing how much a bad quiz score will hurt your average, or frantically trying to figure out what you need on the final exam to keep an 'A', this tool maps out the mathematics for you automatically.

It features two distinct computational modes:
1.  **Weighted Class Grade:** Calculate your current running average based on homework, quizzes, tests, and participation, each carrying different weights.
2.  **Final Exam Calculator:** Work backward. Input your current grade, your desired final grade, and the weight of the final exam to determine exactly what score you need to achieve your goal.

### How Weighted Grades Work
Many classes do not value all assignments equally. A massive mid-term exam might be worth 30% of your total grade, while a weekly homework assignment is only worth 5%. 

To find your true standing, our system multiplies each assignment’s grade by its percentage weight, accumulating the total sum, and dividing it by the total weight of all assignments completed so far. This reveals your exact current percentage, even if the class is only halfway through the semester.

### The Final Exam Formula
Calculating what you need on the final exam uses an algebraic formula:
`Needed Score = (Target Grade - Current Grade × (1 - Final Weight)) / Final Weight`

Example: If your current grade is **85%**, you want a target of **90%**, and your final is worth **20%** (0.20):
*   *(90 - 85 × (1 - 0.20)) / 0.20*
*   *(90 - 85 × 0.80) / 0.20*
*   *(90 - 68) / 0.20 = 110%*
In this scenario, to reach a 90% overall class grade, you would need to score 110% (extra credit) on the final. Validating this *before* finals week is why this tool is vital for academic planning!
