## What is a Daily Calorie Calculator?
Understanding how many calories your body naturally burns is the foundational step to any health, fitness, or diet transformation. 

Our **Online Calorie Calculator** uses the highly accurate **Mifflin-St Jeor Equation** to calculate your physical Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR), and then applies recognized kinetic multipliers to find your Total Daily Energy Expenditure (TDEE).

### How to use this Calculator:
1.  **Enter your biometrics:** Input your exact age, gender, height, and weight. You can strictly toggle between Imperial (US) and Metric units.
2.  **Estimate your Activity Level:** Select the option that best mirrors your average week. *Note: Overestimating activity level is the #1 reason calorie deficits fail.*
3.  **Read your Target Output:** The engine instantly outputs your exact caloric targets categorized by your objective:
    *   **Maintain Weight:** The absolute caloric baseline you need to eat per day to stay exactly the weight you are now.
    *   **Weight Loss:** A standardized ~500 calorie deficit prescribed for healthy, consistent fat loss (approx. 1 lb or 0.5 kg a week).
    *   **Weight Gain:** A standardized ~500 calorie surplus for individuals looking to gain weight or build muscle mass (bulking).

### The Math (Mifflin-St Jeor)
The calculator determines your BMR (the calories you burn if you slept for 24 hours):
*   **Men:** `(10 × weight in kg) + (6.25 × height in cm) - (5 × age) + 5`
*   **Women:** `(10 × weight in kg) + (6.25 × height in cm) - (5 × age) - 161`

Your BMR is then multiplied by an activity factor ranging from `1.2` (Sedentary) to `1.9` (Extremely Active) to provide your final caloric requirement.
