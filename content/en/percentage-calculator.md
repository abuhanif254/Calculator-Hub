## Comprehensive Percentage Calculations
Percentages are arguably the most widely used mathematical operation in daily life—whether you are calculating discounts, analyzing financial growth, computing tax rates, or measuring statistical variance.

Our **Percentage Calculator** provides three distinct, specialized engines on a single interactive view to accommodate every percentage scenario:

### 1. Basic Percentage ("What is X% of Y?")
This engine calculates the exact portion of a whole. 
*   *Example:* What is 20% of 150? (Answer: 30)
*   *Use Case:* Calculating a 20% tip on a $150 restaurant bill, or finding the value of a discount coupon.

### 2. Relative Percentage ("X is what % of Y?")
This engine calculates the ratio between two numbers and converts it into a percentage format.
*   *Example:* 45 is what percent of 180? (Answer: 25%)
*   *Use Case:* Calculating a test score (e.g., you scored 45 out of 180 points), or finding the ROI percentage.

### 3. Percentage Change (Increase / Decrease)
This engine calculates the difference between an old value and a new value over time.
*   *Example:* From $40 to $50. (Answer: 25% Increase)
*   *Use Case:* Finding out how much a stock jumped in price, calculating inflation on a grocery item, or measuring traffic drops on a website.

Our system instantly handles double-precision floating-point arithmetic securely on the client-side to ensure zero data transmission latency and no precision loss.
