## What is a Pace Calculator?
A **Pace Calculator** is an essential tool for runners, cyclists, and athletes of all levels. It allows you to mathematically determine how fast you need to travel per mile or kilometer to achieve a specific finish time for a race, or conversely, what your final finish time will be if you hold a specific pace.

Our **Online Pace Calculator** handles the complex time-math so you don't have to. You can seamlessly calculate:
1.  **Your Pace:** If you know how far you ran and how long it took, find out exactly how fast you were moving per mile or kilometer.
2.  **Your Finish Time:** If you know your target distance (e.g., a Marathon) and your average pace, perfectly predict your finish time.
3.  **Your Distance:** If you ran for a specific duration at a specific pace, calculate exactly how much ground you covered.

### Useful Reference Distances
When training or logging miles, it is helpful to know exact race distances:
*   **5K:** 3.10686 miles (5 Kilometers)
*   **10K:** 6.21371 miles (10 Kilometers)
*   **Half-Marathon:** 13.1094 miles (21.097 Kilometers)
*   **Full Marathon:** 26.2188 miles (42.195 Kilometers)

### How Pace Math Works
Pace is simply the ratio of Time divided by Distance. Because time operates in base-60 (minutes/seconds) rather than base-10 metrics, calculating pace by hand can be extremely frustrating. Our algorithmic engine normalizes all inputs into absolute seconds, executes the geometric ratios, and perfectly reformats the physical output back into readable hours, minutes, and seconds.
