## Managing Your Consumption Taxes
Whether you are a consumer managing a household budget, a traveler navigating foreign shopping rates, or a small business owner issuing invoices, consumption taxes like **Sales Tax, Value-Added Tax (VAT), and Goods & Services Tax (GST)** are unavoidable.

Our **Global Sales Tax Calculator** handles both standard and reverse calculation methods perfectly, ensuring you always know the exact net price, gross price, and isolated tax amount.

### The Two Modes of Calculation
There are two primary ways you will interact with sales tax math:
1.  **Add Tax (Exclusive Pricing):** Common in the United States. The sticker reads $100, but a 7% sales tax is added at the register, bringing the actual total (Gross Price) to $107. Our calculator processes this standard forward projection.
2.  **Extract Tax (Inclusive Pricing / Reverse Tax):** Common in Europe (VAT) and Australia (GST). The sticker reads $100, meaning the tax is *already inside* that figure. If the VAT is 20%, you cannot simply subtract 20% from $100. Our calculator uses the true inverse calculation formula: `Net Price = Gross Price / (1 + Rate)` to correctly reveal that the pre-tax price was actually $83.33, and the tax paid was $16.67.

### Why You Need Exact Extrapolation
For business owners, understanding how to extract your VAT/GST is legally critical for correctly filing your quarterly tax returns. Failing to use the reverse calculation correctly will lead to overpaying or underpaying federal tax authorities. This tool ensures mathematical accuracy across all global percentages.
