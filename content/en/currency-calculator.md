## What is a Currency Calculator?
A **Currency Calculator** is an indispensable tool for international travelers, business professionals, and investors. It allows you to convert the value of one currency into another using current foreign exchange (forex) rates.

Unlike static measurements (like distance or weight), currency values fluctuate constantly based on global economic conditions, market demand, and geopolitical events. Our calculator gives you a reliable way to gauge the relative value of your money anywhere in the world.

### Supported Currencies
Our tool supports all major global currencies, including:
1.  **USD (US Dollar):** The world's primary reserve currency.
2.  **EUR (Euro):** The official currency of the Eurozone.
3.  **GBP (British Pound):** The oldest currency still in continuous use.
4.  **JPY (Japanese Yen), CAD (Canadian Dollar), AUD (Australian Dollar):** And many more.

### Why Exchange Rates Matter
Whether you are budgeting for a vacation abroad, pricing goods for international customers, or simply sending money to family overseas, knowing the exact exchange rate ensures you don't overpay or lose out on hidden fees. This calculator aims to provide an accurate baseline before you make transactions through banks or currency exchanges.
