### 3. Flipping vs. Rotating: Understanding the Difference

It is crucial to understand the difference between an **image rotator** and an **image flipper**. Rotating an image turns it around a central pivot point (like turning a steering wheel). Flipping an image creates a mirror reflection. 

If you have an image with text and you flip it horizontally, the text will read backwards. If you rotate an image 180 degrees, the text will be upside down but will still read left-to-right. Our tool provides dedicated buttons for flipping along the X-axis (Horizontal) and Y-axis (Vertical), ensuring you achieve exactly the transformation you need.
